About Me

I am an Automotive Mechanical Engineer with ten years experience in Computer Assisted Design.

While a student, I won first place -in a team of two- at in-house Faculty of Mechanics Scientific Contest and successfully completed an internship at Renault Technology Romania design offices helping to build a software tool in MatLab/Simulink.

I further built my career at Power Train Divisions of two of the most well known OEMs in Europe as a CAD Engineer. I help in designing complex aluminium high pressure die cast engine/drivetrain components.

I am now looking further to advancing a Full Stack Web Developer career aiming for smart applications and a better technological integration.
